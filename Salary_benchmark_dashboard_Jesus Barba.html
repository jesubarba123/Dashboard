International Salary Benchmark - Tech Roles

Comparison of monthly and annual salary ranges in USD for international remote hiring into U.S.-based employers, considering bilingual candidates with at least 4 years of relevant experience. Includes filters by client, position, country, seniority, work modality, currency, and data confidence level.

Assumptions: Client names have been anonymized as client_1, client_2, etc. Gross salaries in USD, international remote contractor modality, bilingual candidates with at least 4 years of experience, U.S.-based employer, employee countries: Peru, Mexico, Colombia, Costa Rica, Argentina, Chile, Honduras, Guatemala, India, Brazil, and Pakistan. Ranges are approximate and combine salary guides, public platforms, and market judgment. A 10% adjustment was applied to reflect the market premium for bilingual candidates with 4+ years of experience. Honduras, Guatemala, India, Brazil, and Pakistan were added as estimated average expected salary benchmarks for international remote talent; Honduras and Guatemala have lower confidence due to more limited comparable public data.
